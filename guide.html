ABOUT OUR APP

MARAVILLOSAS CASA APP IS HELPING PEOPLE A LOT!!

Maravillosas Casa is an app for helping students, working women, working gents, boys or girls, or families to find a home or PG or hostel to stay or live. In this app, we have two logins one for owners and another for people who are in search of accommodation. Owners login and post their ads about to-let homes or PGs or hostels, based on which the entire details of it would be mentioned in the ad post and on the other side, people who are in need of accommodation can log in and view these ads or filter out the ads based on their priority and contact the owner for further details and get a place to live. So, this app is very user-friendly and any child can also use it and get the information of to-let houses or PGs.
